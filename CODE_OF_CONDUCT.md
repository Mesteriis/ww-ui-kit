# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

This project aims to provide a respectful, technically rigorous, and inclusive collaboration space.
Contributors are expected to communicate clearly, critique ideas rather than people, and keep the
repository safe for maintainers and users.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Assume good intent, but keep technical standards high.
- Accept feedback and course-correct quickly.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the repository.
- Report security and conduct problems responsibly.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or intimidation
- Personal attacks or discriminatory language
- Doxxing or publishing private information
- Deliberate disruption, trolling, or bad-faith escalation
- Sexualized language or imagery in repository spaces

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, reject contributions, or block participation when
behavior violates this code of conduct.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, open a private maintainer contact channel through repository security/contact
information instead of escalating publicly.
